## Workflow Intent

Turn a feature idea into a structured backlog: one epic, delivery-sized stories, and architect-reviewed technical tasks. Writes story files to the workspace context and creates or updates the feature.md entry.

## Execution

Load and follow: `~/.nakiros/workflows/2-pm/plan-feature/workflow.yaml`
